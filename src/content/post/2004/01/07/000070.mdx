<P>　　昨天报纸上看到个有趣的逻辑推理题，爱因斯坦声称世界上只有2%的人能解出：</P>
<UL>
<LI>有五个具有五种不同颜色的房间排成一排； 
<LI>每个房间里分别住着一个不同国籍的人； 
<LI>每个人都在喝一种特定品牌的饮料，抽一特定品牌的烟，养一特定的宠物； 
<LI>没有任意两个人在抽相同品牌的香烟，或喝相同品牌的饮料，或养相同的宠物。</LI></UL>
<H3>问题：谁在养鱼作为宠物？</H3>
<P>爱因斯坦给出如下线索：</P>
<OL>
<LI>英国人住在红色的房子里； 
<LI>瑞典人养狗作为宠物； 
<LI>丹麦人喝茶； 
<LI>绿房子紧挨着白房子，在白房子的左边； 
<LI>绿房子的主人喝咖啡； 
<LI>抽Pall Mall牌香烟的人养鸟； 
<LI>黄色房子里的人抽Dunhill牌香烟； 
<LI>住在中间那个房子里的人喝牛奶； 
<LI>挪威人住在第一个房子里面； 
<LI>抽Blends牌香烟的人和养猫的人相邻； 
<LI>养马的人和抽Dunhill牌香烟的人相邻； 
<LI>抽BlueMaster牌香烟的人和啤酒； 
<LI>德国人抽Prince牌香烟； 
<LI>挪威人和住在蓝房子的人相邻； 
<LI>抽Blends牌香烟的人和喝矿泉水的人相邻。</LI></OL>

<H3>Einstein's riddle</H3>
<UL>
<LI>There are 5 houses in five different colors in a row. 
<LI>In each house lives a person with a different nationality. 
<LI>These five owners drink a certain type of beverage, smoke a certain brand of cigar and keep a certain pet. 
<LI>No owners have the same pet, smoke the same brand of cigar or drink the same beverage.</LI></UL>
<H4>The question is: Who owns the fish?</H4>
<H3>Einstein's hints</H3>
<OL>
<LI>The Brit lives in the red house. 
<LI>The Swede keeps dogs as pets. 
<LI>The Dane drinks tea. 
<LI>The green house is on the immediate left of the white house. 
<LI>The green house's owner drinks coffee. 
<LI>The person who smokes Pall Mall rears birds. 
<LI>The owner of the yellow house smokes Dunhill. 
<LI>The man living in the center house drinks milk. 
<LI>The Norwegian lives in the first house. 
<LI>The man who smokes Blends lives next to the one who keeps cats. 
<LI>The man who keeps horses lives next to the man who smokes Dunhill. 
<LI>The owner who smokes BlueMaster drinks beer. 
<LI>The German smokes Prince. 
<LI>The Norwegian lives next to the blue house. 
<LI>The man who smokes Blends has a neighbor who drinks water.</LI></OL>
